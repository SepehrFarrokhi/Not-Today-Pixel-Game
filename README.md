What makes it hype?

The Shop Strategy: Do you spend your points on raw speed, or do you save up for the game-changing Nova Blast and Missile Support?

Boss Diplomacy: These aren't just bullet sponges. The bosses in Not Today use tactical orbital strikes, spawn reinforcements, and teleport to keep you on your toes.

The Flow: With built-in screen shake, hit flashes, and a custom particle system, every kill feels rewarding and every narrow escape feels legendary.
